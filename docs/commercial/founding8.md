## What is License-to-Impact?

License-to-Impact (L2I) is a unique commercial model that ties enterprise software licensing directly to social impact funding. Every L2I bundle purchased:

1. **Funds Real Learners:** 100% of L2I revenue goes to TEEI's educational programs
2. **Tracks Impact:** Your company receives detailed impact metrics and learner stories
3. **Earns Recognition:** Exclusive Founding-8 badges and public acknowledgment
4. **Aligns Values:** Embed impact into your procurement decisions

## How L2I Bundles Work

### 1. Purchase

**Via Platform:**
1. Navigate to `/cockpit/[companyId]/billing`
2. Select "L2I Bundles" tab
3. Choose tier (Foundation, Growth, Expand, Launch)
4. Select program tags (Language, Mentorship, Upskilling, WEEI)
5. Complete payment

**Via Sales:**
- Contact sales@teei.io for custom bundles
- Enterprise-scale commitments (Launch tier)
- Multi-year agreements

### 2. Allocation

**Manual Allocation:**
- Admin allocates learners via TEEI Platform
- Specify program tag, cohort, learner details
- Track allocation against capacity

**Automatic Allocation:**
- TEEI assigns learners from waitlist

### 3. Tracking

**Dashboard Access:**

**Renewal Benefits:**

## FAQs

**Q: Can I choose which learners receive funding?**
A: No. TEEI assigns learners based on need and program fit. You receive anonymized progress reports.

**Q: What if I don't use all my learner capacity?**
A: Unused capacity expires after bundle validity period (3-5 years). Consider donating unused slots to TEEI's general fund.

**Q: Can I purchase multiple bundles?**
A: Yes! Volume discounts apply (10% for 2+, 15% for 3+).

**Q: Does L2I replace my subscription plan?**
A: No. L2I bundles are add-ons. You still need an active subscription (Essentials/Professional/Enterprise).

**Q: Can I downgrade from Founding-8?**
A: Founding-8 status is permanent once earned. Even if you don't renew, you retain legacy recognition.

**Q: What happens if TEEI discontinues a program?**
A: Learners are migrated to equivalent programs. Your impact credits remain valid.

**Q: Can I visit learners or programs?**
A: Founding-8 members can opt into "Learner Meet & Greet" events (subject to privacy consent).

**Q: How do I report L2I impact to stakeholders?**
A: Use the Impact Dashboard to generate PDF reports. API access available for custom integrations.
