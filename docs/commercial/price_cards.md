## Program Allocation Breakdown

When you purchase an L2I bundle, you can allocate funding across TEEI programs:

### Language Learning
English proficiency courses for non-native speakers to unlock career opportunities

### Mentorship (Buddy System)
1:1 mentor matching connecting learners with experienced professionals

### Upskilling (Kintell)
Career development and technical skills courses for underrepresented talent

### WEEI (Women's Economic Empowerment)
Specialized programs supporting women entrepreneurs and professionals

**Default Allocation** (customizable):
- Language: 40%
- Mentorship: 30%
- Upskilling: 30%
- WEEI: 0% (included in Expander and Launcher tiers)

## FAQ

**Q: Can I change my plan mid-cycle?**
A: Yes! Upgrades are prorated and applied immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.

**Q: How do L2I bundles work with my subscription?**
A: L2I bundles are add-ons to any subscription tier. They're billed separately and fund TEEI programs directly.

**Q: Can I customize my L2I program allocation?**
A: Yes! You can adjust the allocation percentages across Language, Mentorship, Upskilling, and WEEI (if eligible).

**Q: What happens if I exceed my AI token limit?**
A: Overage tokens are billed at $0.005/1K tokens at the end of your billing period.

**Q: Is there a free trial?**
A: Yes! All plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required.

**Q: Can I get a demo before purchasing?**
A: Absolutely! Book a personalized demo with our team: [sales@teei.org](mailto:sales@teei.org)

**Q: What's included in the Founding-8 program?**
A: Founding-8 members (L2I-LAUNCH purchasers) get exclusive benefits including Impact Advisory Board access, co-marketing, custom initiatives, and dedicated CSM support.
