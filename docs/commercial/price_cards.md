## FAQ

**Q: Can I switch plans?**
A: Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades at the next billing cycle.

**Q: What happens if I exceed my plan limits?**
A: We'll notify you at 80%, 90%, and 100% usage. Overages are billed at fair-use rates (see above).

**Q: Are L2I bundles tax-deductible?**
A: Consult your tax advisor. L2I bundles fund TEEI's 501(c)(3) educational programs.

**Q: Can I allocate L2I learners to multiple programs?**
A: Yes, choose program tags when purchasing a bundle (subject to tier availability).

**Q: What's the cancellation policy?**
A: Cancel monthly plans anytime (prorated refunds). Annual plans are non-refundable but can be transferred.
