## Product Overview

**Language for Refugees** is an English proficiency program that provides online courses, assessments, and career-focused language training to unlock employment opportunities for non-native speakers.

**Tagline**: *"From Language Barrier to Career Opportunity"*

---

## The Problem

**72% of refugees cite language barriers as the #1 employment obstacle** (UNHCR, 2024)
- Cannot pass job interviews (English proficiency required)
- Cannot read job postings, fill applications
- Cannot communicate with colleagues, managers

**Result**: 58% unemployment for refugees with low English proficiency vs. 28% with intermediate+ English

---

## The Solution

**Language for Refugees** provides:
- **Online English Courses**: Self-paced, career-focused curriculum (A1 → C1 CEFR levels)
- **Proficiency Assessments**: Pre/post tests to measure progress
- **Career English**: Industry-specific vocabulary (tech, healthcare, retail)
- **Interview Prep**: Mock interviews, pronunciation practice

**Outcome**: 82% achieve intermediate+ English proficiency within 6 months; 64% employed within 9 months

---

## How It Works

### 1. Proficiency Assessment
- **Intake Test**: CEFR level assessment (A1 beginner → C2 proficient)
- **Learning Plan**: Personalized curriculum based on current level + job goal

**Example**:
```
Learner: Amina (Afghan refugee, A2 level, goal: Healthcare Assistant)
Plan: General English (A2→B1) + Healthcare English + Interview Prep
Duration: 6 months, 10 hours/week
```

### 2. Online Courses
- **General English**: Grammar, vocabulary, reading, writing, speaking
- **Career English**: Industry modules (tech, healthcare, retail, finance)
- **Soft Skills**: Email writing, phone etiquette, meeting participation

**Platform Features**:
- Video lessons (20-30 min/module)
- Interactive exercises (quizzes, flashcards)
- Speech recognition (pronunciation practice)
- Progress dashboard

### 3. Impact Measurement
- **Proficiency Gain**: 82% achieve +1 CEFR level (e.g., A2 → B1) in 6 months
- **Employment Rate**: 64% employed within 9 months (vs. 28% baseline)
- **Salary Lift**: $8K avg increase (vs. low-English-proficiency roles)
- **VIS**: 82 (Volunteer Impact Score – 82% positive career outcomes)

**SROI Calculation** (Q2Q AI):
```
Economic Value: $8K salary lift × 64% employment × 3 years = $15,360
Program Cost: $400 (course access + tutor support)
SROI: $15,360 / $400 = 38.4
```
