## Welcome to TEEI!

## First Login

### Accessing Your Tenant

1. Open the invitation email from TEEI
2. Click **"Activate Account"** link
3. You'll be redirected to: `https://[your-company].teei.io/activate`
4. Create your password:
   - Minimum 12 characters
   - At least one uppercase letter
   - At least one number
   - At least one special character
5. Click **"Create Account"**

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

For security, all users must enable 2FA:

1. After creating password, you'll see the 2FA setup screen
2. Choose your method:
   - **Authenticator App** (recommended): Google Authenticator, Authy, 1Password
   - **SMS**: Receive codes via text message
   - **Email**: Receive codes via email

**Setting up Authenticator App**:
1. Open your authenticator app
2. Scan the QR code shown on screen
3. Enter the 6-digit code from your app
4. Click **"Verify"**
5. Save backup codes in a secure location

**Important**: Keep your backup codes safe! You'll need them if you lose access to your 2FA device.

## Dashboard Orientation

After logging in, you'll see the main dashboard.

### Quick Wins - What to Do First

#### 1. Understand Your Period (30 seconds)

Look at the top of the dashboard - you'll see the current period displayed (e.g., "November 2024").

**Quick Actions**:
- Click the period dropdown to view previous months/quarters
- Try "Q3 2024" to see quarterly data
- Click "Year to Date" for annual overview

#### 2. Review Your Top KPIs (2 minutes)

Four main KPI cards are displayed:

**SROI (Social Return on Investment)**:
- Shows the value created per dollar invested
- Higher is better
- Example: "3.2x" means $3.20 of value per $1 invested

**VIS (Volunteer Impact Score)**:
- Measures volunteer effectiveness (0-100)
- Based on engagement, outcomes, and feedback
- 80+ is excellent

**Integration Score**:
- Tracks participant integration (0-1 scale)
- Measures belonging, confidence, social connections
- 0.75+ indicates strong integration

**Participants**:
- Total active participants in current period
- Click for completion rates and cohort breakdown

**Pro Tip**: Hover over any KPI card to see a sparkline trend. Click the card to drill into details.

#### 3. Explore a Chart (2 minutes)

Scroll down to the charts section:

**Trend Chart**:
- Shows how metrics change over time
- Hover over data points for exact values
- Use the legend to toggle metrics on/off

**Program Breakdown**:
- See which programs contribute most to impact
- Compare programs side-by-side
- Identify best performers

**Outcome Distribution (Radar Chart)**:
- Five dimensions: Confidence, Belonging, Language, Job Readiness, Well-being
- Larger area = better outcomes
- Use to identify strengths and gaps

#### 4. Generate Your First Report (5 minutes)

Let's create a simple report:

1. Click **"Generate Report"** in the navigation
2. Select period: Last month
3. Keep default options
4. Click **"Generate"**
5. Wait 30-60 seconds
6. Review the AI-generated narrative
7. Click **"Export PDF"** to download

**Congratulations!** You've just generated your first TEEI report.

## Understanding Your Role

Different roles have different permissions:

### Admin

**You can**:
- View all dashboards and reports
- Manage users (add, edit, delete)
- Configure tenant settings
- Schedule reports
- Access export audit logs

**Key responsibilities**:
- Onboard new team members
- Configure integrations
- Monitor platform usage
- Ensure compliance

### Company User

**You can**:
- View dashboards and reports
- Generate reports (10 per hour limit)
- Export data to CSV/PDF
- View evidence (redacted)

**Key responsibilities**:
- Monitor program performance
- Generate reports for stakeholders
- Analyze trends and insights

### Compliance Officer

**You can**:
- All Company User permissions
- Access export audit logs
- View full evidence lineage
- Generate compliance reports

**Key responsibilities**:
- Ensure data governance
- Monitor exports for compliance
- Prepare audit documentation

### Read-Only Viewer

**You can**:
- View dashboards only
- No report generation
- No data export

**Key responsibilities**:
- Stay informed on program outcomes
- Review high-level trends

## Common First-Time Questions

### "Where is my data?"

Data comes from:
- **Program integrations**: Buddy feedback, Language assessments, Check-ins
- **Partner systems**: Benevity, Goodera, Workday (if configured)
- **Manual uploads**: CSV imports (ask your admin)

**First-time setup**: It may take 24-48 hours after account activation for historical data to appear.
